Bishop

Bishop is a black German Shephard, and is considered to be a rare because of a recessive gene.

  • Scientific Name: German Shephard
  • Age: 9 years old
  • Charateristics: Loyal and Goofy
  • Favorite Activity: Playing in the snow (and eating snow).

Bishop was born with chocolate brown fur with a white patch going down his chest. As he started to shed, the fur started to turn jet black. Now that he's getting older, his fur is starting to revert back to chocolate brown (and gray patches). He went through his wild puppy phases, and terrible teenage phases up until 4 years old. It was torture, but he's finally calm. He's the best dog in the world.
